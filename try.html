Resume

Elisabeth Kaufhold first studied recorder with Birgit Beyer, Prof. Peter Thalheimer, and Prof. Jeremias Schwarzer at the Musikhochschule in Nuremberg from 1998 to 2002. There she also began studying the baroque bassoon with Ursula Bruckdorfer, which she deepened in postgraduate studies at the Schola Cantorum in Basel with Donna Agrell and in Frankfurt with Christian Beuse. At the same time, she began postgraduate studies in recorder at the Musikhochschule in Zurich with Matthias Weilenmann.
